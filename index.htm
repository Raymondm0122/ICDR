Research & Partnership

Institute of Corporate Directors Research Center research and development portfolio aims to enable clear understanding of national imperatives and the needs of industry to optimise the impact of Institute of Corporate Directors Research Center research and development outputs. It leverages public, private, and international partnerships in support of cutting-edge science, engineering, and technology.

We have clients in both the private sectors (micro, small, medium, and large enterprises; formal and informal organisations), as well as in the public sector (national, provincial, and local government). The organisation also deals with public enterprises and institutions. Institute of Corporate Directors Research Center aims to contribute to the national programme of development, perform relevant knowledge generating research and transferring technology and skilled human capital, and strengthening science and technology base.

In partnership with Multi Skill Academy UK faculty of Economic and management Science and our research network will be conducted in partnership.Personal Prospective: Research suggest that, the most powerful way to affect the lives and welfare of public and private institutions is through the improved understanding of what happens as we practice each day, this will foster transparency and accountability at work. Our training development therefore, will focus on development of teaching materials and systems to be used for helping participants gain appropriate understanding and empirical knowledge to help in the development of their different institutions; and will also assist participants in their future endeavours hence corporate governance and better life and economy for all.
